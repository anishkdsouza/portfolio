ANISH D'SOUZA

Anish is a senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Statistics and Analytics and a Bachelor of Arts in Public Policy, with a minor in Data Science. With a passion for using data to drive meaningful insights, he has experience conducting both quantitative and qualitative research to tackle complex policy issues. His work spans from analyzing large datasets to uncover trends and inform decision-making to crafting compelling visual narratives that bring data to life. Beyond his analytical skills, Anish's creative mindset allows him to approach problems with fresh perspectives, making his storytelling through data both impactful and accessible. He is particularly interested in using data analytics to inform public policy decisions and drive positive change.
